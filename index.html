Services

Someday helps you build a coherent culture. Everyone within your company holds a crucial position, weather you are CEO or the janitor, all are needed to reach the vision, but does everyone feel like that? Using our platform we will help you business define and create those actions that builds engagement across the organization. We all need to start somewhere, but we believe that the best start is now.

Onboarding

Building a coherent culture starts when we sign on a new employee. The excitement and the curiosity for who we are and who are the people that work there. Let's give the best start possible, let them get to know you from the inside from the start, so when the first day of work is finally there they feel at home.

Culture

We know that 70% of all processes of changes fail, how can we change that. We believe that we need to continuously train to be able to achieve our goals. Using a gamified platform to motivate and engage the whole company on its strategy and vision.

Retail

We have created an efficient tool for retail when it comes to campaign management. With this feature you can easily send out campaign information and receive photos from the stores. As campaign manager you can easily give feedback and keep a good dialogue with the stores.
